About Jimmy John's Menus

Jimmy John's Menus is an independent, editorially-run guide to Jimmy John's prices, calories, nutrition, hours, locations, and current promotions. We built this site for one reason: to give people a fast, accurate answer without wading through outdated forum posts or conflicting price lists.

This site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Jimmy John's Franchise, LLC. We are an independent publisher covering publicly available menu and restaurant information.

What We Cover

Every guide on this site is built around the same core categories: the full sandwich, wrap, and sides & drinks menus; a complete nutrition breakdown by item; typical store hours and holiday schedules; how Jimmy John's locations and franchising work; and an honest look at which promo codes are worth trying versus skipping.

How We Research

We start from publicly available menu boards, nutrition guides, and franchise information, then cross-check prices and figures across multiple locations where possible, since Jimmy John's is a franchise system and individual stores set their own pricing. We revisit each guide periodically to catch menu changes, new items, and pricing shifts.

Where a number is genuinely an estimate rather than an official figure — a price range instead of one fixed number, for example — we say so directly on the page rather than presenting it as exact.

A Note on Accuracy

Prices, hours, availability, and menu items vary by location and change over time. We do our best to keep every guide current, but the Jimmy John's app, jimmyjohns.com, or a call to your local store will always be the most accurate source for your specific order.

Questions or Corrections?

If you spot something that's out of date or incorrect, we'd like to know. Visit our Contact Us page to get in touch.

Editorial Standards

We regularly review publicly available menu, nutrition, and restaurant information to keep this guide as accurate as possible. Prices may vary by location. Last updated July 2026.

Jimmy John's Locations: How to Find a Store, Hours, Delivery Zones, and What to Expect (July 2026)

This page covers everything tied to finding and using a Jimmy John's location: how many stores exist, how to locate the closest one, what services it offers, how delivery zones work, typical hours, and how ownership and franchising affect what you'll see store to store. Figures below reflect publicly reported data as of July 2026.

Store count, hours, delivery radius, and available services all vary by location, so treat the numbers here as national averages. Always confirm specifics for your address through the official store locator before you order.

Quick Answer

Jimmy John's operates more than 2,700 restaurants across the United States, with additional locations in Canada, Mexico, El Salvador, South Korea, and the United Arab Emirates. About 98% of U.S. stores are independently owned franchises rather than corporate-run. The fastest way to find one is the store locator at jimmyjohns.com or the Jimmy John's app, both of which show real-time hours, delivery zones, and pickup options for each address.

Illinois carries the heaviest concentration of stores by a wide margin, a legacy of the brand's Midwest roots near Eastern Illinois University, where the first location opened. From there, the chain expanded through franchising rather than company-owned growth, which is why store hours, building styles, and even some menu availability can shift from one address to the next.

Three Ways To Look

How to Find the Nearest Jimmy John's

Three tools cover nearly every situation:

Official Store Locator

jimmyjohns.com/locations. Enter a ZIP code or street address and the site returns nearby stores ranked by distance, along with each one's hours, phone number, and whether delivery or pickup is currently available at that address.

Jimmy John's App

Uses your phone's location to surface the closest stores automatically. It also saves a "home store" so repeat orders default to the same location without re-entering an address.

Google Maps or a GPS App

Searching "Jimmy John's" pulls up pins with hours and a phone number, though for delivery eligibility and same-day holiday hours, the official locator or app is more reliable since franchise owners update those directly.

A location showing up on a map doesn't guarantee delivery to your address. Delivery eligibility is checked separately once you enter your exact address in the app or on the site.

Services By Store

What a Jimmy John's Location Offers

Most stores are built around fast counter service rather than a full dine-in restaurant model, though seating is available at the majority of locations.

Dine-in

Standard at most stores, with counter ordering and a small seating area. Some newer or renovated locations skip seating entirely and operate as pickup and delivery only.

Pickup

Order ahead through the app or website and grab your sandwich at the counter, often ready within a few minutes given the chain's "freaky fast" prep model.

Delivery

Handled in-house by store employees rather than gig-economy drivers, within a limited radius of each restaurant. Jimmy John's has also partnered with DoorDash since 2020 for self-delivery listings and DashPass, meaning some orders placed through DoorDash are still fulfilled by the store's own drivers rather than a DoorDash courier.

Curbside

Offered at select locations. Calling ahead or noting curbside in your app order lets staff bring food out to your car.

Catering

Many locations handle box lunches and party platters for office or event pickup, usually with a shorter lead time than most sandwich chains because of the simple menu.

Why Delivery Zones Are So Small

Jimmy John's has historically limited delivery to roughly a five-minute drive from each store. This is a deliberate choice rather than a limitation of coverage. The company has framed its in-house delivery model as central to keeping bread from going stale and produce from wilting in transit, and for years resisted broader third-party delivery for that reason before eventually adding a DoorDash partnership.

Practically, this means someone can live one mile from a location and fall just outside its delivery boundary while a customer half a mile away in a different direction qualifies. The only way to know for certain is entering your address directly in the app or website rather than assuming based on straight-line distance.

States and Countries With Jimmy John's

Jimmy John's has locations across the large majority of U.S. states, concentrated most heavily in the Midwest and expanding steadily through the Sun Belt and coasts as franchisees open new units. A handful of states and territories, including Alaska and Hawaii, currently have no locations.

Outside the U.S., Jimmy John's operates in:

Canada Mexico El Salvador South Korea United Arab Emirates

International units are typically run by regional franchise groups, so menu pricing, item availability, and hours differ more noticeably from the U.S. experience than they do between individual American stores.

Hours of Operation

Hours vary by owner and by market, but most U.S. locations follow a similar general pattern:

WeekdaysTypically open by mid-morning through late evening
WeekendsSimilar hours, sometimes trimmed slightly on Sundays
College-area and downtown storesFrequently extend later into the night to cover late study sessions and after-work orders
HolidaysHours shift store by store, and some locations close entirely on major holidays

Because franchise owners set their own hours within brand guidelines, the listed hours on the store locator or app are more current than a general rule of thumb, especially around holidays. See the full Jimmy John's Hours guide for typical weekday, weekend, and holiday schedules.

JJ Rewards at Your Local Store

Jimmy John's retired its former Freaky Fast Rewards program in late 2025 in favor of JJ Rewards, a straightforward points system. Every dollar spent earns 10 points, which can be redeemed toward menu items rather than waiting on randomized surprise perks. Points apply the same way at any participating U.S. location as long as the order is placed through the app or a logged-in account on the website; in-store orders without scanning the app don't count toward the balance.

Franchise vs. Corporate-Owned Locations

Roughly 98% of Jimmy John's restaurants are franchised, meaning an independent owner or operating group runs day-to-day business under the Jimmy John's brand standards. Inspire Brands, which also owns Arby's and Buffalo Wild Wings, has owned Jimmy John's since 2019 and sets national menu, pricing frameworks, and marketing, while franchisees handle hiring, local hours, and store-level execution. This structure explains most of the small differences customers notice between locations, from slightly different hours to which limited-time items a given store chooses to push.

Jimmy John's Menu Overview

Jimmy John's keeps its menu tight compared to most sandwich chains: a handful of numbered Originals, a longer list of club-style Favorites, a permanent lineup of toasted hot subs, and a few rotating wraps. Everything is built to order on fresh-baked bread, so the same core proteins and cheeses show up across several categories in different combinations.

How the menu is organized (Originals, Favorites, Toasted, Plain Slims, Little Johns, Gargantuan)

Originals are the seven founding sandwiches, numbered 1 through 6 plus the J.J.B.L.T., each dressed with lettuce, tomato, and mayo. Favorites take those same proteins and stack two or three together into heavier club sandwiches. Toasted Sandwiches are a newer, permanent category of hot pressed subs with melted cheese. Plain Slims strip an Original down to just meat and cheese, Little Johns shrink an Original to a 6.5-inch mini portion, and the J.J. Gargantuan sits at the opposite end as a five-meat sandwich built for serious appetites.

Bread & Size Options (French, 9-Grain Wheat & Unwich®)

Most sandwiches can be built on 8-inch or 16-inch French bread, sliced 9-grain wheat, a garlic herb or flour wrap, or as an Unwich, meaning lettuce replaces the bread entirely. The Unwich is the go-to option for low-carb or gluten-sensitive orders, since it drops the bread's added carbs to less than 1 gram. Little Johns are the exception, they're only offered on their own smaller French bread and aren't available as a wrap or Unwich at most locations.

Jimmy John's Menu Prices vs. Subway, Jersey Mike's & Firehouse Subs

Jimmy John's sits in the middle of the sandwich-chain price range. Its standard sandwich runs 8 inches, longer than Subway's 6-inch default but shorter than a 12-inch footlong, and its 16-inch Giant undercuts a full second footlong on a per-inch basis.

Recent side-by-side pricing checks have generally found Subway to be the cheaper option per item, since its menu leans on 6-inch builds and value-tier footlongs, while Jimmy John's leans on a larger standard size and fewer low-price entry points. The trade-off across all three comes down to portion size versus per-item cost: Jimmy John's gives you more sandwich per dollar than Jersey Mike's, but Subway typically undercuts both chains on a straight price comparison.

By The Numbers

Jimmy John's Calories & Nutrition Guide

Calorie counts below reflect the 8-inch French bread version of each sandwich, per Jimmy John's official nutrition guide. Swapping to a wrap, wheat bread, or Unwich changes the total, sometimes significantly. See the full Jimmy John's Nutrition Guide for a complete breakdown by item.

Calories by Sandwich Category (Originals, Favorites & Toasted)

Originals run from 480 calories (Turkey Tom) to 670 calories (Veggie) on 8-inch French bread. Favorites run higher, from 680 calories (Bootlegger Club) up to 1,080 calories (J.J. Gargantuan). Toasted Sandwiches run the highest as a category, from 850 calories (Meatball Marinara) to 1,200 calories (3 Cheese Chicken Melt), largely because of the melted cheese and richer sauces used across that lineup.

Highest and Lowest Calorie Items

The J.J. Gargantuan is the single highest-calorie item on the standard menu at 1,080 calories on 8-inch French bread and 2,160 calories on the 16-inch Giant. On the low end, the Jumbo Kosher Dill Pickle sits at just 20 calories, and among full sandwiches, the Turkey Tom Unwich is one of the lightest options at 250 calories.

Protein, Carbs & Sodium Breakdown

Protein tops out with the J.J. Gargantuan at 78g on 8-inch French bread, followed closely by the Hunter's Club at 55g. Carbohydrates track closely with bread size, an 8-inch French bread sandwich typically carries 47g to 53g of carbs before any wrap or Unwich swap. Sodium climbs fastest on the Italian-style clubs and the Gargantuan, several of which cross 2,000mg per sandwich on 8-inch French bread and can exceed 3,000mg on the 16-inch Giant size.

Low-Carb

Jimmy John's Unwich® (Low-Carb) Menu Guide

What Is an Unwich® & How Many Calories and Carbs Does It Save?

An Unwich replaces the bread on any sandwich with lettuce, dropping the added carbs from the bread to less than 1 gram and cutting overall calories substantially since the bread itself typically accounts for 200 to 470 calories depending on size. A Turkey Tom, for example, goes from 480 calories on 8-inch French bread down to 250 calories as an Unwich.

Best Unwich® Picks for Keto & Low-Carb Diets

The Turkey Tom Unwich (250 calories, 5g carbs) and Big John Unwich (270 calories, 4g carbs) are among the lowest-carb full sandwiches on the menu. The Billy Club Unwich, one of the six official Protein Packed items, adds 37g of protein at just 300 calories, making it one of the better low-carb, high-protein combinations available.

Is Jimmy John's Gluten-Free? (Cross-Contamination Explained)

Jimmy John's doesn't offer a dedicated gluten-free bread, so the Unwich is the standard substitute for anyone avoiding gluten. However, Jimmy John's own allergen guide notes that shared prep surfaces, utensils, and slicers mean it cannot guarantee any item is free of cross-contact with wheat or gluten, so the Unwich reduces but doesn't eliminate risk for people with celiac disease or a severe gluten sensitivity.

Off-Menu Builds

Jimmy John's Secret Menu

Jimmy John's doesn't run an official secret menu, but its full customization system makes a few off-menu builds possible at most locations. A toasted Totally Tuna with a double portion of provolone gives the tuna salad a melted, richer finish that isn't available by ordering it plain. Ordering an Italian Night Club with added hot peppers and avocado spread turns an already loaded sandwich into a spicier, creamier version some regulars order by request rather than by name. A Plain Slim 6 with double provolone, toasted, works as an unofficial “cheese sub” for anyone who wants something simple and cheap. None of these are guaranteed at every store since ingredient availability, like hot peppers or sprouts, varies by location.

Feeding A Group

Jimmy John's Catering Menu & Party Box Pricing

Party Box Sizes & Per-Person Cost

Jimmy John's catering centers on party platters and boxed lunches. An 18-piece party box typically serves up to 9 people, while a 30-piece box, made from 10 full sandwiches cut into thirds, typically serves 10 to 15 people and generally runs somewhere in the $65 to $110 range depending on the sandwiches chosen and your location. Boxed lunches, which include an individual sandwich, chips, a pickle, and a cookie per person, generally run $8 to $14 per person. Pickles usually aren't included in party platters to keep the bread from getting soggy, so it's worth adding a pickle side separately if you want them.

How to Order Jimmy John's Catering Online

Catering can be ordered through the Jimmy John's website or app by selecting the catering option and entering your delivery address and headcount. Jimmy John's generally recommends at least 24 hours' notice for orders feeding more than 20 people, and calling your local store directly is the safer route for same-day catering, since online ordering doesn't always reflect a store's real-time kitchen capacity.

Ordering Smart

How to Get the Best Jimmy John's Prices

Ordering Direct vs. DoorDash, Uber Eats & Grubhub

Ordering directly through the Jimmy John's app or website is typically cheaper than ordering the same sandwich through a third-party delivery app, since platforms like DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub add their own service and delivery fees on top of the menu price. Third-party apps can be worth it when they're running their own promo codes or when a location is outside Jimmy John's own delivery radius but still covered by the third-party driver network.

Active Promo Codes & the $8.99 Meal Deal

The $8.99 Meal Deal, covering the Pepe, Turkey Tom, and Vito with chips and a drink, is the most consistent nationwide value option right now and doesn't require a promo code. Beyond that, promo codes tend to be seasonal and market-specific, often distributed through the Jimmy John's app, email list, or third-party delivery platforms rather than posted permanently on the main site.

Freaky Fast Rewards® Program

Freaky Fast Rewards is Jimmy John's free loyalty program, earning points on every purchase that convert into free food. New members typically receive an offer for a free regular Original sandwich after their first qualifying purchase of $5 or more, and the program includes a birthday reward as long as your birthday is set in your account. Sign-up happens through the app or website, and points track automatically once you're logged in at checkout.

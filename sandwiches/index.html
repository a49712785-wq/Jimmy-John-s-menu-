Jimmy John's Sandwich Menu: Every Sandwich, Price, and Calorie Count (July 2026)

Jimmy John's built its entire reputation on one product: the sandwich. No fryer, no rotating hot bar — just fresh-baked bread, meat sliced daily, and hand-cut veggies stacked to order. This guide breaks down every sandwich on the menu, organized the way Jimmy John's organizes it in-store: Originals, Favorites, Toasted Sandwiches, Little Johns, Plain Slims®, and the oversized J.J. Gargantuan®, plus the sandwich-only combo meals built around them.

A quick note before diving in: Jimmy John's doesn't publish one fixed national price list — each franchise location sets its own pricing, so numbers can shift 15–20% between markets. The prices below are typical national ranges pulled from current menu data, meant for budgeting rather than as a guaranteed quote. Confirm the exact price for your store on the app or at checkout.

Every sandwich below can be ordered on French bread, sliced 9-grain wheat, or as an Unwich® (a lettuce-wrap version with no bread at all), and in Little John (6.5"), Regular (8"), or Giant (16") sizes unless otherwise noted.

Full Meal, One Line

Sandwich Combo Meals

Jimmy John's builds its combos around the sandwich you've already chosen rather than offering a separate fixed combo menu. Sandwich-specific combo options are:

Original Combo

Any regular Original sandwich (#1–#6), paired with a side and drink.

Favorite Combo

Any regular Favorite sandwich (#7–#17 or the J.J.B.L.T.), paired with a side and drink.

Toasted Combo

Any regular Toasted Sandwich, paired with a side and drink.

Turning a sandwich into a combo typically adds a few dollars to the base sandwich price. It's the standard way to order a full meal in one line without picking sides and drinks separately.

Prices, calories, and availability are current as of July 2026 and are subject to change by location. For the complete Jimmy John's menu — including wraps, sides, drinks, desserts, catering, and the JJ Rewards™ program — see our full Jimmy John's Menu guide.

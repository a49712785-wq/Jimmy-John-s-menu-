Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Jimmy John's Menus (jimmyjohnsmenus.us) collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. Effective date: July 2026.

Information We Collect

We don't require an account or login to use this site. We may collect basic, non-identifying technical information automatically, such as your browser type, device type, approximate location, and pages visited, typically through analytics tools. If you contact us directly, we collect whatever information you choose to share, such as your name and email address.

Cookies and Analytics

This site may use cookies or similar technologies from analytics and advertising providers to understand how visitors use the site and, where applicable, to serve relevant ads. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; doing so may affect how parts of the site display.

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used to operate and improve this site, understand which guides are most useful, and respond to messages sent through our Contact Us page. We don't sell personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services, such as analytics or advertising providers, that collect information under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party service linked from this site.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes take effect once posted on this page, along with an updated effective date.

Questions about this policy? Visit our Contact Us page.

Editorial Standards

We regularly review publicly available menu, nutrition, and restaurant information to keep this guide as accurate as possible. Prices may vary by location. Last updated July 2026.

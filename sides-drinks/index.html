Jimmy John's Drinks & Sides Menu: Prices, Calories, and Every Option (July 2026)

This page breaks down every drink and side Jimmy John's sells on its own, separate from the sandwiches themselves. You'll find fountain and bottled drink options, chip flavors, pickles, and the two cold salads, each with pricing, calorie counts, and what's actually in them as of July 2026.

Prices and exact sizes shift a little by market and by franchise owner, so treat the numbers below as a solid national baseline. Combo pricing, where a chip bag and drink get bundled with a sandwich, is usually cheaper than buying each item separately.

Chips, Pickles & Cold Salads

Sides

Jimmy John's keeps its side menu short on purpose: two chip-based options, one cold cut classic, and two made-from-scratch salads. Nothing here is toasted or hot, in keeping with the brand's cold-sandwich identity, though the salads rotate more than people expect.

Fountain & Bottled

Drinks

Jimmy John's runs on Coca-Cola beverage products at the fountain, plus a small bottled lineup for anyone grabbing a drink to go. Fountain sizing varies slightly by location depending on the cup and machine each franchise uses, generally landing in a 22 ounce regular and a 30 to 32 ounce large.

Ordering Sides and Drinks Together

Adding a side and a drink to a sandwich individually usually costs more than upgrading to a combo, which typically bundles a bag of chips and a regular fountain drink with any sandwich or wrap for a small flat add-on. If you already know you want both, checking the combo price before ordering each item separately is worth the extra few seconds.

Final Thoughts

The sides and drinks menu at Jimmy John's stays deliberately small, four chip flavors, one pickle, two cold salads, and a Coca-Cola-based drink lineup. That simplicity keeps ordering fast, which lines up with the brand's whole "freaky fast" approach. For the full sandwich lineup these pair with, check the main Jimmy John's menu guide.

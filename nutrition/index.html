Jimmy John's Nutrition Menu (July 2026): Calories, Protein & Healthy Choices

This guide covers the complete Jimmy John's nutrition menu: calories, protein, carbs, fat, and sodium for every sandwich, wrap, side, dessert, and drink on the current lineup. Use it to compare items fast, spot the lightest options, and build a meal around your goals, whether that means cutting carbs, hitting a protein target, or just eating a bit cleaner.

Every number below comes from Jimmy John's official nutrition data. Actual values can shift slightly depending on bread choice, added toppings, sandwich size, or your specific location, so treat these figures as a reliable baseline rather than an exact match every time.

The Big Picture

Quick Nutrition Overview

Across the menu, calories range from as low as 70 (a plain Slim 4, Turkey) up to over 2,400 for a Giant Gargantuan. A few patterns hold across almost every category:

Bread Is the Biggest Driver

Regular French bread alone runs roughly 390–790 calories depending on size. Ordering as an Unwich® removes nearly all of it.

Mayo Adds Up

A regular serving adds around 160–330 calories and 16–33 grams of fat depending on sandwich size.

Protein Scales With Size

Most Regular sandwiches land between 20–50 grams of protein; Giants roughly double that.

Sodium Tracks With Cured Meats

Salami, capocollo, and bacon push sodium well above turkey or roast beef, especially in the toasted lineup.

Lowest-Calorie Jimmy John's Items

  1. Slim 4, Turkey Breast (Regular): the lowest-calorie sandwich on the menu at 460 calories with bread included
  2. Little John 4, Turkey Tom®: 240 calories, the lightest full-veggie option
  3. Little John 2, Big John®: 250 calories with 28g of protein, a strong calorie-to-protein ratio
  4. Any sandwich as an Unwich®: removes 300–800 calories depending on size, since it eliminates the bread entirely
  5. Jumbo Kosher Dill Pickle: 20 calories, essentially free as a side

Highest-Protein Menu Items

  1. J.J. Gargantuan® (Giant): 159g of protein, the highest on the menu
  2. Spicy East Coast Italian® (Giant): 112g of protein
  3. Hunter's Club® (Giant): 113g of protein with comparatively lower fat than other Giants
  4. Ultimate Italian, Toasted (Giant, estimated): roughly 137g of protein
  5. Beach Club® (Giant): 93g of protein alongside a lighter fat profile than most other Favorites

Pairing a high-protein Favorite or the Gargantuan with an Unwich® is the most efficient way to raise protein-per-calorie on the menu.

Best Low-Carb & Unwich® Choices

Ordering any sandwich as an Unwich® cuts carbs by roughly 90–95% since it removes the bread entirely, the single largest carb source on the menu. For the lowest absolute carb count, start from a naturally lean base:

  1. Big John® or Turkey Tom® as an Unwich®: minimal fillings beyond meat, lettuce, tomato, and mayo, so carbs come almost entirely from the vegetables
  2. Any Slim as an Unwich®: since Slims already skip veggies and condiments, removing the bread leaves an almost carb-free sandwich of meat and cheese
  3. Vito® as an Unwich®: the oil and vinegar dressing adds flavor without the carb load of a creamy sauce

Vegetarian Nutrition Guide

The Veggie (#6) is the only meatless item on the numbered sandwich menu: 730 calories, 29g protein, 60g carbs, and 39g fat at Regular size, driven mostly by the double provolone and avocado spread. Slim 6 (Provolone & Avocado Spread) is the vegetarian Slim option, at 630 calories and 31g protein for a Regular on French bread. Both can be ordered as an Unwich® to reduce the carb and calorie load significantly while keeping the same protein from the cheese.

Jimmy John's Allergen Information

Per Jimmy John's official allergen guide:

Peanuts

Sandwich ingredients don't contain peanuts or tree nuts, but Jimmy Chips® are cooked in 100% peanut oil. Some locations operate as shared kitchens where peanut, shellfish, and tree nut allergens are present.

Gluten/Wheat

There is no gluten-free bread. The Unwich® is the standard substitution, though Jimmy John's notes its kitchens aren't dedicated gluten-free facilities, so cross-contact is possible.

Dairy

Provolone and cheddar are the obvious sources, but ranch dressing, ranch seasoning, and all three desserts also contain milk.

Egg

The Hellmann's® mayo used across the menu contains egg. Mustard and oil and vinegar are egg-free alternatives.

Fish/Shellfish

Present in the Totally Tuna® and Club Tuna® sandwiches specifically; not used elsewhere on the sandwich menu.

Jimmy John's recommends speaking with in-store staff about any specific allergy before ordering, since prep procedures can vary slightly by location.

Before You Order

Nutrition Tips Before You Order

Skip or Swap the Mayo

It's the single biggest fat and calorie add-on across the entire menu; yellow mustard or oil and vinegar carry a fraction of the calories.

Go Unwich® for Anything Carb-Sensitive

It's the single biggest lever on the menu for cutting both carbs and total calories.

Little John Over Regular

If you're calorie-conscious but still want bread, it roughly halves calories, carbs, and fat while keeping the same flavor profile.

Watch the Sodium

Several Giant-size Favorites and Toasted Sandwiches exceed 4,000mg of sodium, well above a full day's recommended intake in a single meal.

Add Protein Without Adding Much Fat

Turkey and roast beef are the leanest ways to boost protein; salami, capocollo, and bacon add more saturated fat per gram of protein.

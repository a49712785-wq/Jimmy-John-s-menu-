Jimmy John's Hours: Opening, Closing & Holiday Schedule (July 2026)

Quick Answer

Most Jimmy John's locations open between 10:00 AM and 10:30 AM and close between 9:00 PM and 10:00 PM, seven days a week. Since every Jimmy John's is franchise-operated, exact hours shift by city, day of the week, and holiday, so the posted hours at your specific location are always the ones that matter.

This page covers typical weekday and weekend hours, what to expect around major holidays, delivery and catering cutoff times, and the fastest ways to confirm hours for the Jimmy John's nearest you.

Patterns, Not One Nationwide Schedule

Hours below reflect common patterns reported across many locations, not one official nationwide schedule. Always check the Jimmy John's app, the store locator on jimmyjohns.com, or Google Maps for your specific store before heading out.

Typical Weekday Hours

Jimmy John's staff usually arrive early, often around 7:00 AM, to bake bread and prep ingredients before the doors open to customers. Most stores open between 10:00 AM and 10:30 AM Monday through Friday and stay open through the lunch and dinner rush.

Opening10:00 AM–10:30 AM (most locations)
Closing9:00 PM–10:00 PM (most locations)
Busiest windows11:30 AM–1:30 PM and 5:00 PM–7:00 PM

Downtown and business-district locations sometimes open slightly earlier, around 10:00 AM, to catch the early lunch crowd.

Typical Weekend Hours

Saturday hours generally match weekday hours, with many locations opening around 10:00 AM–10:30 AM and closing as late as 10:00 PM or 11:00 PM. Sunday hours run slightly shorter at many stores, often starting around 11:00 AM and closing between 8:00 PM and 9:00 PM.

Saturday10:00 AM–11:00 PM at many locations
Sunday11:00 AM–8:00 PM or 9:00 PM at many locations

Some locations near universities extend Friday and Saturday hours into the late night to serve students, occasionally staying open until 2:00 AM or 3:00 AM.

Holiday Exceptions

Jimmy John's stays open through most minor holidays but closes or shortens hours on a handful of major ones. Franchise owners set the final schedule, so treat this as a general pattern rather than a guarantee for every store.

Plan Ahead

Because hours shift year to year and store to store, call ahead or check the app before planning a visit around Thanksgiving, Christmas, or New Year's.

Late-Night and Early-Morning Locations

Round-the-clock Jimmy John's locations are rare, but a small number of stores near college campuses, hospitals, or downtown entertainment districts extend hours well past midnight, especially on weekends. The store locator and app let you filter for locations with extended or late-night hours near you.

Delivery and Catering Hours

Delivery generally runs during normal store hours and stops shortly before closing, commonly around 30 minutes before the doors lock, to give drivers time to return safely. If your local store closes at 9:00 PM, expect the delivery cutoff to land around 8:30 PM.

Catering orders typically need at least a day or two of advance notice, and some locations offer early drop-off for large office or event orders placed well ahead of time. After-hours catering pickup usually isn't available, so confirm timing directly with your local store when booking.

Final Thoughts

Jimmy John's keeps a fairly predictable schedule nationwide, generally open from mid-morning through mid-evening every day of the week. The exceptions come around major holidays like Thanksgiving and Christmas, when most stores close, and at the small number of late-night locations near campuses and hospitals.

Since hours are set at the franchise level, the safest move before a trip or an order is always a quick check of the app, the store locator, or a phone call to your specific location.

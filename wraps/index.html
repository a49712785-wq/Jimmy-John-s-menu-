Jimmy John's Wraps: Menu, Prices, Calories & Ingredients (July 2026)

Quick Answer

Jimmy John's sells four wraps in July 2026: the Chicken Caesar Wrap, the Kickin' Ranch Chicken Wrap, and the Tuscan Italian Wrap as permanent, year-round items, plus the new Buffalo Ranch Chicken Wrap as a limited-time flavor. Every wrap can be ordered as an Unwich, swapping the flour tortilla for fresh iceberg lettuce, and any wrap can be turned into a combo with chips and a drink.

Jimmy John's rotates wrap flavors more often than its sub lineup, so pricing and availability shift by store and by season.

This page breaks down every wrap Jimmy John's sells right now, along with prices, calories, ingredients, and customization options for each one. You will also find a nutrition comparison table, Unwich swap details, combo pricing, and answers to the most common questions people ask before ordering.

Prices and calories listed below are approximate and can vary by location, portion size, and any customizations you add. Always confirm the current price and nutrition info in the Jimmy John's app or at your local store before ordering.

Unwich: The Lettuce Wrap Option

Every wrap on this list can be ordered as an Unwich, which swaps the flour tortilla for crisp iceberg lettuce. This is the same low-carb format Jimmy John's uses for its subs.

Switching to an Unwich typically cuts a wrap's calories by 250–350 and drops the carbohydrate count from around 60–70 grams down to roughly 15–25 grams. It is a common request from customers following low-carb, keto, or gluten-conscious eating patterns, though the kitchen cannot guarantee a gluten-free environment.

Wrap Combo: Pricing and What's Included

A Wrap Combo pairs any wrap with a side and a 22 oz fountain drink. Side options usually include a bag of Jimmy Chips, a pickle spear, or a cookie.

Allergen Information

Jimmy John's wraps commonly contain wheat, gluten, milk, egg, and soy through the tortillas, cheese, and dressings. Cross-contact with peanuts and tree nuts is possible in stores that prepare other menu items nearby. Anyone with a food allergy should ask staff for current allergen sheets, since recipes and suppliers can change.

Ordering Tips: Pickup vs. Delivery

Wraps travel well because the flour tortilla holds up better over time than French bread, which makes them a solid pick for delivery or catering orders. For the freshest texture, pickup and eat within 15–20 minutes of preparation. Mobile ordering through the Jimmy John's app lets you save customizations and reorder your usual wrap in a few taps.

Final Thoughts

Jimmy John's wraps give you the same fresh ingredients as the sub menu in a lighter, more portable format. The Chicken Caesar, Kickin' Ranch Chicken, and Tuscan Italian wraps anchor the year-round lineup, while the Buffalo Ranch Chicken Wrap adds a limited-time option worth trying before it rotates off the menu.

Whichever flavor you choose, the Unwich option and easy customization make it simple to adjust calories, carbs, or spice level to fit what you're looking for. Check the app for current pricing and availability at your nearest location before you order.
